How It Works

From Vision to Live Website

A simple, transparent process designed to keep you informed and excited at every stage.

01

Discover

We start with a conversation — via WhatsApp or call. I learn about your business, your goals, your target audience, and what success looks like for you. No generic forms. Real listening.

02

Design

Based on our discussion, I design a bespoke layout that aligns with your brand identity and speaks directly to your audience. You'll see a preview before we build anything.

03

Build

Once the design is approved, I build your website with clean code — mobile-responsive, fast-loading, and SEO-ready. You're updated throughout. No radio silence.

04

Launch

Your website goes live. I walk you through everything you need to know, hand over all files and credentials, and stay available for any post-launch support you need.
